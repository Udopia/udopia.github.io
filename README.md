### Profile & Focus
Over 20 years of hands-on software engineering experience -- from bare-metal performance optimization to full-stack system design -- combined with academic expertise in algorithmic complexity. My focus lies on efficient symbolic reasoning (SAT/SMT, planning, optimization) and its increasing integration with machine learning toward neuro-symbolic AI.

**Core Competencies:**
*   **Systems & Performance Engineering:** Bare-metal and low-level optimization, high-performance solver engineering for large-scale combinatorial problems, and parallel algorithm design.
*   **Full-Stack & Architecture:** End-to-end system design spanning bare metal, backends, web frontends, and relational data modeling.
*   **Research & Teaching:** Hands-on and academic expertise in efficient symbolic reasoning (SAT/SMT, planning, optimization), data-driven algorithm development, and trustworthy AI.
